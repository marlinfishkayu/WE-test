    Win Three Cards

    A game to challenge your intelligence.

    Win Three Cards is a popular card game in China. The principal of the game is to predict the outcome of the bet with the highest ranking on the card types. Both “Dragon” and “Phoenix” sides will receive three cards for each round to determine the winner.

    FEATURE HIGHLIGHTS

    Broad selection
    of odds

    This game has a good variety of odds with various wager combinations. Players could feel free to make their choices to win more bets!

    Detailed game
    statistics

    The game clearly demonstrates the past 20 and 100 rounds of winning statistics to help the players with their next bet.

    Good time for
    multi-betting

    Win Three Cards is a fantastic pick for anyone who enjoys multi-betting. The players can place their bets anywhere and even add their bet anytime.
